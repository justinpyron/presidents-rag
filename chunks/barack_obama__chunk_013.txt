Obama met Trinity United Church of Christ pastor Jeremiah Wright in October 1987 and became a member of Trinity in 1992.[107] During Obama's first presidential campaign in May 2008, he resigned from Trinity after some of Wright's statements were criticized.[108] Since moving to Washington, D.C., in 2009, the Obama family has attended several Protestant churches, including Shiloh Baptist Church and St. John's Episcopal Church, as well as Evergreen Chapel at Camp David, but the members of the family do not attend church on a regular basis.[109][110][111]

I'm not that superstitious, so it's not like I think I necessarily have to have them on me at all times."
