Le Duc Thọ in Paris parallel to the official talks that the South Vietnamese were unaware of.[31] In June 1971, Kissinger supported Nixon's effort to ban the Pentagon Papers saying the "hemorrhage of state secrets" to the media was making diplomacy impossible.[32]
