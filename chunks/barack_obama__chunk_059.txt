On December 23, 2016, under the Obama Administration, the United States abstained from United Nations Security Council Resolution 2334, which condemned Israeli settlement building in the occupied Palestinian territories as a violation of international law, effectively allowing it to pass.[178] Netanyahu strongly criticized the Obama administration's actions,[179][180] and the Israeli government withdrew its annual dues from the organization, which totaled $6 million, on January 6, 2017.[181] On January 5, 2017, the United States House of Representatives voted 342–80 to condemn the UN Resolution.[182][183]
