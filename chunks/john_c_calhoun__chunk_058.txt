Legacy
Monuments and memorials
See also: List of places named for John C. Calhoun
Many different places, streets, and schools were named after Calhoun. Some, such as Springfield, Illinois (1832)[1] and Jackson County, Kansas (1859), were subsequently renamed. The "Immortal Trio" (Calhoun, Daniel Webster, and Henry Clay) were memorialized with streets in Uptown New Orleans.
In June 2020, Clemson University removed John C. Calhoun's name from Clemson University Calhoun Honors College, renaming it to Clemson University Honors College.
