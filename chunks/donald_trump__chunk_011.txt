Media career
Main article: Media career of Donald Trump
See also: Bibliography of Donald Trump
Trump has produced 19 books under his name. At least some of them have been written or co-written by ghostwriters.[1] His first book, The Art of the Deal (1987), was a New York Times Best Seller. While Trump was credited as co-author, the entire book was written by Tony Schwartz. According to The New Yorker, the book made Trump famous as an "emblem of the successful tycoon".[2]

Trump had cameos in many films and television shows from 1985 to 2001.[3]
Starting in the 1990s, Trump was a guest about 24 times on the nationally syndicated Howard Stern Show.[4] He had his own short-form talk radio program
