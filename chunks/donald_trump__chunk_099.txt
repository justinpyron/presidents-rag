In a January 2018 discussion of immigration legislation, Trump reportedly referred to El Salvador, Haiti, Honduras, and African nations as "shithole countries".[62] His remarks were condemned as racist.[63][64]
In July 2019, Trump tweeted that four Democratic congresswomen—all from minorities, three of whom are native-born Americans—should "go back" to the countries they "came from".[65] Two days later the House of Representatives voted 240–187, mostly along party lines, to condemn his "racist comments".[66] White nationalist publications and social media praised his remarks, which continued over the following days.[67] Trump continued to make similar remarks during his 2020 campaign.[68]
Misogyny and allegations of sexual misconduct
