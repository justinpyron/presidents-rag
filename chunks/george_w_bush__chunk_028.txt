Stem cell research and first veto
Federal funding for medical research involving the creation or destruction of human embryos through the Department of Health and Human Services and the National Institutes of Health has been forbidden by law since the passage of the Dickey–Wicker Amendment in 1995.[81] Bush has said he supports adult stem cell research and has supported federal legislation that finances adult stem cell research.
