In 2009 he was named to the World Golf Hall of Fame in the Lifetime Achievement category for his contributions to the sport.[26] In 1973, he was inducted into the Hall of Great Westerners of the National Cowboy & Western Heritage Museum.[27] On 27 October 2023, Fort Gordon was redesignated Fort Eisenhower.[a][29][30]
