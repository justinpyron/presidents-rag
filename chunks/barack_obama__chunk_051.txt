The killing led to significant controversy.[124] His teenage son and young daughter, also Americans, were later killed in separate US military actions, although they were not targeted specifically.[125][126]
In March 2015, Obama declared that he had authorized U.S. forces to provide logistical and intelligence support to the Saudis in their military intervention in Yemen, establishing a "Joint Planning Cell" with Saudi Arabia.[127][128] In 2016, the Obama administration proposed a series of arms deals with Saudi Arabia worth $115 billion.[129] Obama halted the sale of guided munition technology to Saudi Arabia after Saudi warplanes targeted a funeral in Yemen's capital Sanaa, killing more than 140 people.[130]
In September 2016 Obama was snubbed by Xi Jinping and the Chinese Communist Party as he descended from Air Force One to the tarmac of Hangzhou International Airport for the 2016 G20 Hangzhou summit without the usual red carpet welcome.[131]
