Final years
Bush supported Republican John McCain in the 2008 presidential election,[19] and Republican Mitt Romney in the 2012 presidential election,[20] but both were defeated by Democrat Barack Obama. In 2011, Obama awarded Bush with the Presidential Medal of Freedom, the highest civilian honor in the United States.[21]
Bush supported his son Jeb's bid in the 2016 Republican primaries.[22] Jeb Bush's campaign struggled, however, and he withdrew from the race during the primaries. Neither George H. W. nor George W. Bush endorsed the eventual Republican nominee, Donald Trump;[23] all three Bushes emerged as frequent critics of Trump's policies and speaking style, while Trump frequently criticized George W. Bush's presidency.
