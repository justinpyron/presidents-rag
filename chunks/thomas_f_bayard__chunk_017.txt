Seward suggested that both countries work together to reduce immigration.[48] Congress passed a new law to that effect, the Chinese Exclusion Act, in 1882.[48] Bayard supported this new act, which became law with President Chester A. Arthur's signature that year.[49]
Election of 1880
Main article: 1880 United States presidential election
As the election of 1880 drew near, Bayard was again regarded as a likely candidate.[50] Hayes had pledged himself to a one-term presidency, which meant the Republicans would not have the advantage of incumbency.
