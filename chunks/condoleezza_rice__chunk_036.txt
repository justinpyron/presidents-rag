However, she added that she has been "concerned about a government role" but has "tended to agree with those who do not favor federal funding for abortion, because I believe that those who hold a strong moral view on the other side should not be forced to fund" the procedure.[9]
Affirmative action
Rice has taken a centrist approach to "race and gender preferences" in affirmative action policies.[11] She described affirmative action as being "still needed," but she does not support quotas.[12]
