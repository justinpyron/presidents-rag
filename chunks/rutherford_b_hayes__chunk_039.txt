In 1877 former President Grant embarked on a world tour shortly after his second term. Hayes was aware of Grant's popularity in Europe and encouraged Grant to extend his tour in the hope that it would improve various foreign relations and strengthen American interests abroad.[61] When Grant was in Nice he boarded USS Vandalia, a screw sloop-of-war that Hayes had personally sent by for Grant's winter cruise about the Mediterranean and  journey to Egypt.[62][63]
