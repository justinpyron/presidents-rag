In January 1938, Nixon was cast in the Whittier Community Players production of The Dark Tower in which he played opposite his future wife, a high school teacher named Thelma "Pat" Ryan.[2] In his memoirs, Nixon described it as "a case of love at first sight",[10] but apparently for Nixon only, since Pat Ryan turned him down several times before agreeing to date him.[11] Once they began their courtship, Ryan was reluctant to marry Nixon; they dated for two years before she assented to his proposal.
