Health
Main article: Health of Abraham LincolnLincoln is believed to have had depression, smallpox, and malaria.[1] He took blue mass pills, which contained mercury,[2] to treat constipation.[3] It is unknown to what extent this may have resulted in mercury poisoning.[4]
Several claims have been made that Lincoln's health was declining before the assassination. These are often based on photographs of Lincoln appearing to show weight loss and muscle wasting.[5] It is also suspected that he might have had a rare genetic disease such as Marfan syndrome or multiple endocrine neoplasia type 2B.[5]
