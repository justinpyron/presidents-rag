2020 presidential campaign
Main article: Joe Biden 2020 presidential campaign
Speculation and announcement
Between 2016 and 2019, media outlets often mentioned Biden as a likely candidate for president in 2020.[1] When asked if he would run, he gave varied and ambivalent answers, saying "never say never".[2] A political action committee known as Time for Biden was formed in January 2018, seeking Biden's entry into the race.[3] He finally launched his campaign on April 25, 2019,[4] saying he was prompted to run because he was worried by the Trump administration and felt a "sense of duty".[5]
