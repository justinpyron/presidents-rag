In February 2021, Obama and musician Bruce Springsteen started a podcast called Renegades: Born in the USA where the two talk about "their backgrounds, music and their 'enduring love of America.'"[23][24] Later that year, Regina Hicks had signed a deal with Netflix, in a venture with his and Michelle's Higher Ground to develop comedy projects.[25]
On March 4, 2022, Obama won an Audio Publishers Association (APA) Award in the best narration by the author category for the narration of his memoir A Promised Land.[26] On April 5, Obama visited the White House for the first time since leaving office, in an event celebrating the 12th annual anniversary of the signing of the Affordable Care Act.[27][28][29] In June, it was announced that the Obamas and their podcast production company, Higher Ground, signed a multi-year deal with Audible.[30][31] In September, Obama visited the White House to unveil his and Michelle's official White House portraits.[32] Around the same time, he won a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Narrator[33] for his narration in the Netflix documentary series Our Great National Parks.[34]
