Business activities
In February 2021, Trump registered a new company, Trump Media & Technology Group (TMTG), for providing "social networking services" to U.S. customers.[13][14] In March 2024, TMTG merged with special-purpose acquisition company Digital World Acquisition and became a public company.[15] In February 2022, TMTG launched Truth Social, a social media platform.[16] As of March 2023[update], Trump Media, which had taken $8 million from Russia-connected entities, was being investigated by federal prosecutors for possible money laundering.[17][18]
Investigations, criminal indictments and convictions, civil lawsuits
Trump is the only U.S. president or former president to be convicted of a crime and the first major-party candidate to run for president after a felony conviction.[19] He faces numerous criminal charges and civil cases.[20][21]
