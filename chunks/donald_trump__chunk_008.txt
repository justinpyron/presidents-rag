In 2013, the State of New York filed a $40 million civil suit against Trump University, alleging that the company made false statements and defrauded consumers.[56] Additionally, two class actions were filed in federal court against Trump and his companies. Internal documents revealed that employees were instructed to use a hard-sell approach, and former employees testified that Trump University had defrauded or lied to its students.[57][58][59] Shortly after he won the 2016 presidential election, Trump agreed to pay a total of $25 million to settle the three cases.[60]
Foundation
Main article: Donald J. Trump Foundation
The Donald J. Trump Foundation was a private foundation established in 1988.[61][62] From 1987 to 2006, Trump gave his foundation $5.4 million which had been spent by the end of 2006. After donating a total of $65,000 in 2007–2008, he stopped donating any personal funds to the charity,[63] which received millions from other donors, including $5 million from Vince McMahon.[64] The foundation gave to health- and sports-related charities, conservative groups,[65] and charities that held events at Trump properties.[63]
