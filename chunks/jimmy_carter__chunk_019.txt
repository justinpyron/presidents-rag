On July 15, 1976, Carter chose U.S. senator Walter Mondale as his running mate.[20] Carter and Ford faced off in three televised debates,[21] the first United States presidential debates since 1960.[21][22]
For the November 1976 issue of Playboy, which hit newsstands a couple of weeks before the election, Robert Scheer interviewed Carter.
