Syrian civil war
See also: Foreign involvement in the Syrian civil war § United States, and American-led intervention in the Syrian civil war
On August 18, 2011, several months after the start of the Syrian civil war, Obama issued a written statement that said: "The time has come for President Assad to step aside."[197] This stance was reaffirmed in November 2015.[198] In 2012, Obama authorized multiple programs run by the CIA and the Pentagon to train anti-Assad rebels.[199] The Pentagon-run program was later found to have failed and was formally abandoned in October 2015.[200][201]
