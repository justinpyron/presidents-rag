Inauguration
Main article: Inauguration of Joe Biden
Biden was inaugurated as the 46th president of the United States on January 20, 2021.[1][2] At 78, he was the oldest person to have assumed the office.[1][3] He is the second Catholic president (after John F. Kennedy)[4][5] and the first president whose home state is Delaware.[6] He is also the first man since George H. W. Bush to have been both vice president and president, and the second non-incumbent vice president (after Richard Nixon in 1968) to be elected president.[7] He is also the first president from the Silent Generation.[8][9]
