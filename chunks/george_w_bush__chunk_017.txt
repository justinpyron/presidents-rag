Between 2001 and 2008, GDP grew at an average annual rate of 2.125 percent,[16] less than for past business cycles.[17] Bush entered office with the Dow Jones Industrial Average at 10,587, and the average peaked in October 2007 at over 14,000. When Bush left office, the average was at 7,949, one of the lowest levels of his presidency.[18] Only four other U.S. presidents have left office with the stock market lower than when they began.[19]
