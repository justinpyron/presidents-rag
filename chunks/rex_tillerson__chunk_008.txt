Tillerson's use of the alias became publicly known in March 2017, after New York State Attorney General Eric Schneiderman wrote in a letter to a judge that Tillerson had used the "Wayne Tracker" email for at least seven years.[37] Later that month, Exxon revealed that emails from the alias account from September 2014 to September 2015 were missing; a further search recovered some emails, but none between September 5, 2014, and November 28, 2014. An attorney for Exxon said a "unique issue" limited to that account led to emails being automatically deleted.[38][39]
Other affiliations
Tillerson is a trustee of the Center for Strategic and International Studies and the American Petroleum Institute.[40] He is also a member of the Business Roundtable.[1] In 2013, he became a member of the National Academy of Engineering.[41] He was a member of the executive committee of The Business Council for 2011 and 2012.[42]
